Phoenix real estate agent Erik Johnson launched TheBuyerProfile.com, an AI-powered pre-showing tool that scores buyer readiness and helps agents prioritize leads before wasting time on unprepared clients. The subscription service targets high-performing agents seeking systematic approaches to qualify buyers upfront rather than conducting initial consultations during property showings.

---

- **Market Problem**: Experienced agents waste significant time on unqualified leads, with traditional approach of conducting buyer consultations at first property showing proving inefficient and costly
- **AI Integration**: System generates "buyer readiness" scores and key signals from short online forms covering budget, preferences, and financing status, differentiating it from basic lender pre-qualification tools
- **Target Market**: Primarily designed for top 25% of agents seeking systematic lead qualification, applicable across independent agents and large brokerage affiliates
- **Pricing Structure**: Subscription model with three tiers ranging $19-199 monthly, indicating scalable revenue potential in fragmented real estate market
- **Competitive Positioning**: Positioned as CRM complement rather than replacement, filling gap between initial lead capture and first client meeting
- **Early Traction**: Positive initial feedback driving product refinements, suggesting market validation for systematic buyer qualification approach
- **Strategic Implication**: Reflects broader industry trend toward AI-assisted client screening as agents seek efficiency gains in increasingly competitive market
