Anthropic is opening a Sydney office to serve Australia and New Zealand's rapidly growing AI market, where the countries rank 4th and 8th globally in Claude usage per capita, while exploring local compute infrastructure to meet data residency demands from enterprises and government agencies.

---

- **Regional expansion strategy** Fourth Asia-Pacific office joining Tokyo, Bengaluru, and Seoul, reflecting strong enterprise demand and high per-capita Claude adoption rates in ANZ region

- **Established customer base** Already serving major organizations including Canva, Quantium, and Commonwealth Bank of Australia, plus startups in AgTech, climate tech, and physical AI sectors

- **Executive engagement** Leadership team visiting Australia in late March to formalize partnerships with institutions and meet customers and policymakers, signaling high-priority market status

- **Local compute exploration** Investigating Australian infrastructure capacity through third-party partners to address data residency requirements from enterprises and government agencies

- **Democratic AI positioning** Expansion aligns with Anthropic's belief that democracies should lead AI development and Australia's ambitions to become a trusted AI infrastructure destination

- **Usage patterns identified** Strong adoption for computer coding tasks, educational instruction, and research applications, informing local team building and partnership strategies

- **Long-term infrastructure planning** Early-stage conversations about permanent regional infrastructure beyond initial third-party partnerships, with future announcements expected
