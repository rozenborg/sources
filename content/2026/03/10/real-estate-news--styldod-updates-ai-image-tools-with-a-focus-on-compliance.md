Styldod's upgraded ReimagineHome AI now automatically detects and fixes MLS compliance violations in listing photos before publication, with CRMLS embedding the technology directly into its listing workflow to prevent fines and reduce manual corrections. The company is positioning itself as an industry-centric AI provider that prioritizes transparency and regulatory compliance over generic photo enhancement tools.

---

- **Proactive Compliance Fix** CRMLS integration automatically corrects common MLS violations like visible license plates and family photos before listings go live, eliminating reactive enforcement
- **Batch Processing Efficiency** New workflow processes up to 40 photos simultaneously with automated enhancements, reducing agent editing time from 90+ minutes to background processing
- **Decision-Grade Standards** AI maintains room proportions and avoids structural changes to prevent buyer misrepresentation, addressing early AI tool accuracy problems
- **Regulatory Documentation** System generates disclosure reports for AI edits to comply with emerging state regulations like California's AI requirements
- **Market Differentiation** Styldod leverages real estate industry focus against generic AI companies, emphasizing built-in policy controls and MLS-specific compliance features
- **Strategic Partnership Model** CRMLS investment and direct workflow integration demonstrates path for other MLSs to adopt compliant AI tools at scale
- **Consumer Decision Tools** Platform includes budget-based renovation visualization and shoppable product integration for buyer engagement and pre-offer planning
