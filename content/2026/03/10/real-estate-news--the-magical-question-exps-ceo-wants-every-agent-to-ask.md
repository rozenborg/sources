eXp CEO Leo Pareja urges agents to ask leads "How did you hear about me?" after an agent discovered a client found them through ChatGPT's recommendation as the most investor-focused agent in their area. This highlights how AI is reshaping lead generation, making agents' digital presence crucial for surfacing in AI-powered searches rather than traditional SEO optimization.

---

- **AI-driven lead discovery** ChatGPT is now recommending specific agents to consumers based on specializations, requiring agents to optimize content for language learning models rather than just search engines

- **Critical tracking question** Agents must ask "How did you hear about me?" to identify which marketing channels and AI platforms are generating leads, enabling strategic resource allocation

- **Emotional transaction limits** AI can generate leads and streamline processes but cannot replace agents in managing the deeply emotional aspects of real estate transactions worth hundreds of thousands of dollars

- **MLS transparency advantage** The U.S. and Canadian cooperative MLS systems create transparent pricing that Pareja warns could be threatened by major players pushing private listings

- **Market consolidation threat** Three brokerages could control 60% market share within 3-5 years, though Pareja believes smaller players will survive by serving agents in different career phases

- **Consumer experience priority** Pareja advocates for reducing transaction friction over short-term shareholder interests, arguing better consumer experiences create long-term shareholder value
