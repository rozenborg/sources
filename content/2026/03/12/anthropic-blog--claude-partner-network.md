Anthropic is launching a $100 million partner ecosystem program to accelerate enterprise Claude adoption through consulting firms, system integrators, and professional services companies. The company is positioning itself as the most partner-committed AI vendor by providing direct financial support, technical certifications, and dedicated engineering resources to organizations helping enterprises deploy Claude in production environments.

---

- **Major financial commitment** - $100 million initial investment in 2026 with expectations to grow, including direct partner funding for training, sales enablement, and co-marketing initiatives

- **Technical certification launch** - New "Claude Certified Architect, Foundations" exam available today, with additional certifications for sellers and developers rolling out later this year

- **Massive partner scaling** - Anthropic is expanding partner-facing team fivefold and training 30,000+ professionals at major firms like Accenture to meet enterprise demand

- **Code modernization focus** - New starter kit targets legacy system migration and technical debt remediation, addressing one of the highest-demand enterprise use cases for Claude's coding capabilities

- **Multi-cloud advantage** - Claude remains the only frontier AI model available across all three major cloud providers (AWS, Google Cloud, Microsoft), strengthening enterprise deployment options

- **Partner infrastructure buildout** - New Partner Portal provides access to Anthropic Academy training, internal sales playbooks, and Services Partner Directory for enterprise buyer discovery

- **Production deployment emphasis** - Program specifically designed to help enterprises move from proof-of-concept to production deployments with dedicated Applied AI engineers and technical architects
