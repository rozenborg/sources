Enterprises must stop treating AI as merely a technology tool and start viewing it as a fundamental operating system that requires complete business model transformation, warns Publicis Sapient CEO Nigel Vaz. The biggest strategic failures occur when companies pursue linear planning processes and functional silos instead of embracing cross-functional data flows and rapid iteration cycles that AI enables.

---

- **Operating System Shift** AI fundamentally changes how decisions are made and work gets done, similar to the internet's impact in the 1990s, requiring organizations to abandon annual strategy cycles for faster, more responsive planning processes

- **Sweet Spot Selection** Successful AI transformation requires picking problems large enough to be organizationally relevant but small enough to deliver quick value, avoiding both dismissible pilot projects and overwhelming enterprise-wide initiatives

- **Linear Thinking Trap** The most common failure pattern is following traditional step-by-step processes with functional separation instead of designing for interconnected data flows across sales, marketing, manufacturing, and other departments

- **Business-Led Strategy** AI strategy should live at the business level, not in IT departments, focusing on customer value creation and new business processes rather than compute power and model selection

- **Ethical Implementation** Responsible AI requires embedding ethical principles directly into technological systems and decisions, including data sovereignty, model transparency, and employee sandbox environments to prevent inadvertent data exposure

- **Human-AI Coexistence** Leaders must prepare for the evolution from AI tools to AI agents and eventual AI coworkers, developing new management approaches for directing persistent, memory-enabled systems that can operate with increasing autonomy

- **Unit Economics Focus** Strategy validation should measure smallest possible increments like cost per release and cycle time per feature rather than waiting for annual milestone reports, enabling rapid strategic pivots based on real execution data
