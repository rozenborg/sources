Anthropic is launching Claude connectors for major creative software including Adobe Creative Cloud, Blender, Autodesk Fusion, and Ableton Live, enabling AI-assisted workflows within existing professional tools rather than replacing them. The move positions Claude as a creative amplifier that handles repetitive tasks and extends technical capabilities while preserving human creativity and taste.

---

- **Creative tool integration** Eight new connectors launched with Adobe, Blender, Autodesk, Ableton, Splice, and others, allowing Claude to work directly within existing creative software ecosystems
- **Technical capability expansion** Claude can write custom scripts, plugins, and procedural systems for creative software, extending what professionals can accomplish without deep coding knowledge  
- **Workflow automation** AI handles repetitive production tasks like batch processing, asset management, and cross-platform data translation, freeing creatives for higher-value work
- **Real-time performance support** Resolume connector enables live visual artists and VJs to control performance software through natural language during shows
- **Educational partnerships** Collaborating with RISD, Ringling College, and Goldsmiths University to integrate AI tools into creative curricula and gather practitioner feedback
- **Open source commitment** Anthropic joined Blender Development Fund as patron and built connectors on open MCP standard, making them accessible to other LLMs beyond Claude
- **New product launch** Claude Design enables rapid prototyping and iteration on software experiences with export capabilities to tools like Canva
