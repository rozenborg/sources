Anthropic launches Claude Design, a visual collaboration tool powered by Claude Opus 4.7 that enables users to create designs, prototypes, and presentations through conversation, automatically applying team design systems and offering seamless handoffs to development workflows.

---

- **Vision Model Integration** Claude Design leverages Opus 4.7's advanced vision capabilities to transform text descriptions into polished visual work including prototypes, wireframes, pitch decks, and marketing materials
- **Automated Brand Consistency** The platform reads existing codebases and design files during onboarding to automatically build and apply team design systems across all projects, ensuring brand alignment without manual oversight
- **Conversational Refinement** Users can iterate on designs through natural language, inline comments, direct edits, or custom sliders, dramatically reducing traditional design iteration cycles from weeks to single conversations
- **Cross-Platform Workflow** Offers comprehensive import/export capabilities including web capture tools, DOCX/PPTX uploads, and exports to Canva, PDF, HTML, plus direct handoffs to Claude Code for development
- **Enterprise Collaboration** Features organization-scoped sharing with granular permissions, enabling team collaboration within a single interface while maintaining security controls for enterprise deployments
- **Market Positioning** Directly competes with design tools like Figma and Canva by offering AI-native workflows that eliminate traditional design bottlenecks, particularly targeting non-designers in product and marketing roles
- **Technical Accessibility** Enables "frontier design" capabilities including voice, video, shaders, and 3D elements without requiring specialized technical skills, potentially democratizing advanced design creation
