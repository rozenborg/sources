Anthropic secures multi-gigawatt TPU capacity from Google and Broadcom starting 2027, driven by explosive revenue growth from $9 billion to over $30 billion run-rate in 2026. The partnership represents Anthropic's largest compute commitment to date as customer demand accelerates, with over 1,000 enterprise customers now spending $1+ million annually.

---

- **Revenue explosion** Run-rate revenue jumped from ~$9 billion end-2025 to over $30 billion in 2026, indicating 3x+ growth trajectory
- **Enterprise adoption surge** Million-dollar annual customers doubled from 500 to over 1,000 in less than two months, signaling rapid enterprise AI adoption
- **Strategic compute diversification** Multi-cloud approach across AWS Trainium, Google TPUs, and NVIDIA GPUs provides workload optimization and reduces single-vendor risk
- **US infrastructure investment** Majority of new capacity located domestically, expanding $50 billion American computing commitment from November 2025
- **2027 capacity timeline** Multi-gigawatt TPU deployment starting 2027 suggests significant lead times for frontier AI infrastructure scaling
- **Cloud platform ubiquity** Claude remains the only frontier model available across all three major cloud platforms (AWS, Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure)
- **Demand-driven scaling** Infrastructure expansion directly tied to customer growth rather than speculative capacity building
- **Competitive positioning** Massive compute commitment signals intent to maintain frontier AI leadership against OpenAI and other competitors
