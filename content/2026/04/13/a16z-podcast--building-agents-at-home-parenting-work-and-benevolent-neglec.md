Jesse Genet has built 11 AI agents to manage everything from coding and curriculum planning to household tasks while homeschooling four children and running a startup, demonstrating how AI can enable radical lifestyle changes for working parents. Her approach combines sophisticated agent architecture with "benevolent neglect" parenting philosophy to create systems that handle routine decisions and free up cognitive bandwidth for high-value activities.

---

- **Agent Portfolio** Genet operates 11 specialized AI agents covering coding, curriculum planning, household management, and business operations, each with specific roles and logging systems for accountability

- **Architectural Approach** She emphasizes proper agent architecture with clear boundaries, logging mechanisms, and feedback loops rather than simple ChatGPT interactions, treating agents as digital employees with defined responsibilities

- **Parenting Integration** The "benevolent neglect" philosophy allows children more independence while AI handles routine educational and household decisions, freeing parents for strategic guidance and quality time

- **Work-Life Synthesis** AI agents enable her to homeschool four children while running a startup by automating decision-making processes that traditionally consume significant mental energy throughout the day

- **Scaling Framework** Her system demonstrates how AI can handle increasingly complex family and business operations, suggesting a model for other entrepreneur-parents seeking work-life integration

- **Cognitive Load Reduction** By delegating routine choices to AI agents, she preserves mental bandwidth for high-stakes parenting moments and strategic business decisions that truly require human judgment
