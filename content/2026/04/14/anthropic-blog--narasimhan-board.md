Anthropic's Long-Term Benefit Trust has appointed Novartis CEO Vas Narasimhan to its Board of Directors, giving trust-appointed directors majority control of the AI company's governance. This marks a strategic pivot toward healthcare applications and responsible scaling, bringing pharmaceutical industry expertise to guide Anthropic's development of AI systems for medical and life sciences applications.

---

- **Governance shift** Trust-appointed directors now hold majority control of Anthropic's board, strengthening the independent oversight mechanism designed to balance profit with public benefit mission
- **Healthcare expertise** Narasimhan brings direct experience overseeing development and approval of 35+ novel medicines in heavily regulated pharmaceutical industry, critical for AI medical applications
- **Regulatory navigation** Appointment signals Anthropic's focus on responsible deployment at scale, leveraging Narasimhan's track record in one of the world's most regulated industries
- **Strategic direction** Healthcare and life sciences positioned as priority verticals where Anthropic sees greatest potential for AI to improve human life quality
- **Board composition** Narasimhan joins seven-member board including founders Dario and Daniela Amodei, Netflix's Reed Hastings, and other technology leaders
- **Global health alignment** Narasimhan's background in HIV/AIDS, malaria, and TB programs across developing markets aligns with Anthropic's humanitarian AI development goals
- **Institutional credibility** Appointment adds National Academy of Medicine member and former PhRMA chair to board, enhancing credibility with medical and regulatory communities
