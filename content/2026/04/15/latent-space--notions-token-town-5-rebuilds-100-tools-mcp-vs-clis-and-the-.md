Notion rebuilt its AI agent system five times over three years before successfully launching Custom Agents, revealing how the company has evolved from a productivity tool into an agent-native platform for enterprise work. The breakthrough came when they stopped swimming upstream against model limitations and started building the right infrastructure around user workflows like email triaging and data capture, with their meeting notes feature emerging as an unexpected growth engine that feeds the broader system.

---

- **Multiple Rebuild Strategy** - Notion rebuilt their agent system 4-5 times since 2022, failing early due to lack of function calling standards, short context windows, and exposing too much complexity to models before finding success with Claude 3.5 Sonnet

- **Agent Lab Implementation** - Focus shifted from building "cool tools" to understanding specific user journeys like email triaging and PDF export, treating AI development as product expertise around collaboration rather than just model wrappers

- **Engineering Culture Design** - Sarah Sachs runs "low-ego teams" comfortable deleting their own work, with objective-setting over idea ownership and fluid management boundaries that allow engineers to move between the "Simon Vortex" prototyping and productionization phases

- **Evaluation Philosophy** - Three-tier eval system: regression tests, launch-quality evals, and "frontier/headroom" evals that intentionally pass only ~30% of the time to track where model capabilities are heading

- **Software Factory Vision** - Simon Last sees coding agents as "the kernel of AGI" with plans for automated workflows spanning specs, self-verification, bug flows, and agent supervision with minimal human intervention

- **MCP vs CLI Debate** - Team is bullish on CLI tools for their self-debugging capabilities while using MCP selectively, with pricing models based on credits abstracting over tokens, model types, and future sandbox costs

- **Meeting Notes as Data Engine** - Their transcription feature became a major growth loop not just for documentation but as high-signal data capture that powers search, custom agents, and follow-up workflows across the collaboration system

- **Enterprise Agent Composition** - Agents operate through shared Notion databases as primitives, with "manager agents" supervising specialized agents and memory implemented simply as pages and databases rather than complex vector stores
