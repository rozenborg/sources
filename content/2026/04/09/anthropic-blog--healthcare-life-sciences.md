Anthropic launches Claude for Healthcare with HIPAA-ready products and personal health data integrations, while expanding life sciences capabilities to cover clinical trials and regulatory operations. The new Claude Opus 4.5 model shows significant improvements in medical task performance and reduced hallucinations, positioning Claude as a comprehensive AI platform for the entire healthcare and drug development pipeline.

---

- **HIPAA-Ready Healthcare Products** Claude for Healthcare introduces connectors to CMS Coverage Database, ICD-10, and National Provider Identifier Registry, enabling healthcare organizations to automate prior authorization reviews, claims appeals, and care coordination while maintaining regulatory compliance.

- **Personal Health Integration** Individual users can now connect Claude to their health records through HealthEx, Function, Apple Health, and Android Health Connect to understand test results, prepare for appointments, and track health patterns with privacy controls and explicit opt-in requirements.

- **Clinical Trial Operations Support** New integrations with Medidata, ClinicalTrials.gov, and ToolUniverse allow life sciences companies to manage trial enrollment, site performance monitoring, and protocol development with AI assistance covering the entire drug development lifecycle.

- **Model Performance Leap** Claude Opus 4.5 demonstrates substantial improvements on medical task simulations and reduced factual hallucinations compared to previous versions, making it more reliable for high-stakes healthcare and scientific applications.

- **Enterprise Partnerships** Major healthcare and pharma companies including Sanofi, Schrödinger, and Veeva are already deploying Claude across their operations, reporting efficiency gains and faster time-to-market for drug development processes.

- **Multi-Cloud Availability** Claude remains the only frontier AI model available across AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure, supported by partnerships with major consulting firms to accelerate enterprise adoption in regulated industries.
