Circle is building Arc Blockchain as an "operating system" for AI agents to store value and execute contracts autonomously, addressing a critical gap as traditional banking infrastructure cannot support machine-driven economic activity. CEO Jeremy Allaire predicts this convergence of AI and programmable money will drive double-digit GDP growth as the global economy becomes increasingly automated.

---

- **Banking infrastructure gap** Traditional banks operate on decades-old systems incompatible with AI agents that need to execute thousands of micro-transactions and smart contracts autonomously in real-time

- **Stablecoins as internet money** USDC and similar dollar-backed tokens provide internet-native currency that AI agents can use for seamless global transactions without traditional banking intermediaries or forex complications

- **Arc Blockchain positioning** Circle's new blockchain specifically designed for the "agentic economy" where AI agents interact economically, featuring built-in compliance, programmable money, and smart contract capabilities

- **Asset tokenization acceleration** Real-world assets from treasury bills to real estate will be tokenized on blockchain, creating liquid markets that AI agents can access and trade programmatically

- **Full-reserve banking shift** Traditional fractional reserve banking will give way to full-reserve models backed by tokenized assets, reducing systemic risk while enabling programmable financial services

- **Economic growth projection** Allaire forecasts sustained double-digit GDP growth as AI agents automate economic processes and blockchain infrastructure reaches its "broadband moment" of mass adoption

- **Privacy and compliance integration** Advanced cryptographic techniques will enable AI agents to transact privately while maintaining regulatory compliance through built-in reporting and audit trails
