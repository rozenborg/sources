Traditional hiring processes are failing because generative AI allows any candidate to create polished résumés and deliver structured interview responses regardless of their actual competence. The fundamental signals companies have relied on for decades—presentation skills and interview performance—are now infinitely scalable and practically free to manufacture.

---

- **Signal degradation** - Core hiring indicators like résumé quality and interview articulation no longer correlate with candidate competence due to AI-assisted preparation
- **Scalability crisis** - What once required genuine skill development can now be generated instantly, flooding hiring pipelines with artificially enhanced applications
- **Cost advantage shift** - The traditional premium placed on communication skills and presentation becomes meaningless when AI can replicate these capabilities for any candidate
- **Verification imperative** - Companies must pivot toward skill-based assessments, work samples, and practical demonstrations that cannot be easily AI-generated
- **Process redesign urgency** - Organizations still relying on traditional screening methods risk systematically selecting candidates based on AI capability rather than job performance potential
- **Competitive disadvantage** - Companies slow to adapt their hiring practices will lose top talent to competitors who can better identify genuine competence beyond AI-enhanced presentations
