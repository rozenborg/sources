Anthropic has secured a massive enterprise AI deployment through DXC Technology, which will embed Claude into the mission-critical systems running major banks, airlines, and government agencies worldwide. DXC will train tens of thousands of certified engineers to integrate Claude across regulated industries, after proving the AI's capabilities by using it to generate 95% of the code for their new OASIS platform.

---

- **Massive Scale Deployment** DXC will train tens of thousands of "forward-deployed engineers" certified through Anthropic Academy to integrate Claude into enterprise systems across 70 countries and 115,000+ employees

- **Mission-Critical Validation** DXC used Claude to build 95% of their OASIS AI-native platform code, speeding development by 10x and proving Claude's reliability under strict security and compliance requirements before customer rollouts

- **Four Strategic Verticals** Initial deployment focuses on insurance modernization, legacy system refactoring (MaaS), cybersecurity operations centers, and application maintenance services where Claude can deliver immediate value

- **Regulatory Environment Penetration** The alliance specifically targets highly regulated industries (banking, airlines, insurance, government) that have been slower to adopt AI due to compliance requirements

- **Competitive Moat Building** By embedding certified engineers directly inside customer organizations, Anthropic creates switching costs and deeper integration than typical software vendor relationships

- **Enterprise AI Maturation Signal** DXC's willingness to stake its reputation on Claude for decades-old client relationships indicates enterprise confidence in AI reliability has reached a tipping point

- **Revenue Model Expansion** The Claude Partner Network approach suggests Anthropic is moving beyond direct sales to channel partnerships for enterprise market penetration
