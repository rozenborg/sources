Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella is positioning Microsoft as the "Frontier Intelligence Platform" where customers can build their own specialized AI systems using multi-model harnesses, private evaluations, and enterprise context layers, fundamentally shifting the company from selling software products to enabling companies to create their own frontier-level AI capabilities. He predicts that private evaluations and agent interaction traces will become the most valuable intellectual property for enterprises, potentially requiring new accounting standards as "token IP" goes on balance sheets alongside traditional assets.

---

- **Platform Strategy Evolution** - Microsoft is moving beyond single models to become an ecosystem platform where companies can build "frontier intelligence" using multi-model harnesses, private evaluations, and enterprise context layers like Work IQ

- **MAI Models & Clean Lineage** - Microsoft's new MAI models focus on "clean lineage" pre-training with high-quality data and ablation studies, enabling smaller 5B models to outperform larger ones through hill-climbing scaffolds and specialized training

- **Private Evals as New IP** - Companies' private evaluation datasets and agent interaction traces will become their most valuable intellectual property, allowing them to improve any frontier model while maintaining competitive advantage

- **Real-World Deployment Complexity** - The biggest underestimation was the complexity of deploying AI for measurable real-world value rather than just benchmark performance, leading to "token maximization" concerns when value isn't clearly demonstrated

- **SaaS Model Transformation** - Traditional SaaS applications must unbundle their data models, business logic, and UI layers to work with agents, with Microsoft's M365 serving 10-100x more usage through Work IQ exposing previously captive enterprise data

- **Pricing Model Flexibility** - Future pricing will blend per-user subscriptions, consumption-based models, and potentially outcomes-based pricing, with enterprises preferring predictable budgets until they achieve outcomes, then resisting revenue sharing

- **Build vs Buy Recalibration** - Enterprises are experiencing "agent euphoria" and attempting to rebuild applications internally, but will likely return to vendor solutions after one budget cycle when maintenance costs and complexity become apparent

- **Human-Token Capital Synthesis** - The future enterprise will combine human capital with "token capital" (AI agents), with the interaction traces between humans and agents becoming valuable training data for company-specific AI systems
