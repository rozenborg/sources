Anthropic is formalizing its partner ecosystem with a three-tier Services Track that measures firms based on certified practitioners, production deployments, and customer endorsements, while launching a Partner Hub portal that gives partners real-time visibility into their standing and helps enterprises find qualified Claude integration specialists.

---

- **Three-tier partner structure** Select (10+ certified individuals, 2+ production customers), Preferred (100+ certified, 15+ customers), and Global Premier (1,000+ certified, 100+ customers across 3+ regions) tiers based on actual Claude deployment experience rather than firm size

- **Major enterprise adoption momentum** Six global consulting giants are deploying Claude at massive scale - Accenture training 30,000 professionals, Cognizant rolling out to 350,000 associates, and Deloitte, KPMG, PwC reaching hundreds of thousands of users each

- **Partner Hub dashboard integration** Real-time tracking portal with daily updates on tier standing and MCP connector allowing partners to query their status directly through Claude conversations

- **Predictable advancement framework** Tier promotions processed twice yearly (January 1, July 1) with transparent requirements, 90-day notice before any tier reductions, and separation between practice-building metrics and business referral rewards

- **$100 million partner investment** Funding dedicated to training, technical support, and shared marketing, with over 40,000 firms applying and 10,000+ consultants already Claude-certified since March launch

- **Quality-focused credentialing** Individual certifications earned through Anthropic Partner Academy exams, requiring active Claude usage within 90 days, ensuring partners have hands-on experience before client deployment

- **Customer validation requirements** Public customer stories and production deployment metrics create accountability and help enterprises identify partners with proven Claude integration track records
