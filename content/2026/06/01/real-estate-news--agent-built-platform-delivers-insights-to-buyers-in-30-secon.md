HouseMe.ai, created by The Agency brokers, launches June 1 in Toronto as a free platform that delivers comprehensive property analysis including valuations, negotiation strategies, and cost breakdowns within 30 seconds. Rather than replacing agents, the tool aims to generate highly qualified leads by removing information barriers and demonstrating clear buyer intent before connecting users with human brokers.

---

- **Market Timing** Toronto launch coincides with 19.3% year-over-year sales decline, targeting information-hungry buyers in a challenging market where data-driven decisions become more critical

- **Core Functionality** Platform combines four tools: instant Intelligence Reports, True Cost Calculator with closing breakdowns, AI Valuation Scores (0-10 rating), and Negotiation Strategy recommendations based on days-on-market and comparable sales

- **Technical Infrastructure** Built on 224,000+ MLS records with 95% accuracy threshold, processes natural language queries in under one second across 97 languages, targeting both domestic and international buyers

- **Business Model** Free consumer access designed to capture qualified buyer intent and route leads to premium agents, eliminating traditional $500 appraisal barriers while monetizing through lead generation

- **Competitive Positioning** Agent-created tool positions human brokers as enhancement rather than replacement, using AI to demonstrate value before client engagement rather than competing with agent services

- **Expansion Strategy** U.S. rollout planned within months following Toronto pilot, leveraging celebrity broker Peter Torkan's "Luxe Listings Toronto" profile for market penetration

- **Lead Quality Focus** Platform filters serious buyers by providing sophisticated analysis upfront, ensuring agents receive prospects who have already engaged with detailed property data and investment analysis
