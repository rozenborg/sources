Anthropic has confidentially filed draft S-1 paperwork with the SEC for a potential IPO, marking a major milestone for the AI company that recently raised $65 billion at a $965 billion valuation. This filing gives Anthropic the regulatory pathway to go public when market conditions align, potentially creating the largest AI-focused public offering to date.

- **Valuation Baseline** Recent $65B Series H funding at $965B post-money valuation provides reference point for potential public market pricing
- **Strategic Positioning** IPO option gives Anthropic flexibility to access public capital markets amid intense AI competition with OpenAI, Google, and others
- **Product Portfolio** Recent Claude Opus 4.8 launch demonstrates continued model advancement in coding and agentic tasks ahead of potential public debut
- **Global Expansion** New Milan office opening reflects international growth strategy that could attract European institutional investors
- **Market Impact** Potential listing would create first major pure-play AI public company, setting valuation benchmarks for the sector
- **Regulatory Compliance** Filing under Rule 135 maintains strict securities law compliance while signaling serious IPO intent
