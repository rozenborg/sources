## Summary

- Anthropic officially opens second Asia office in Bengaluru, led by Managing Director Irina Ghose
- India represents second-largest market for Claude.ai with run-rate revenue doubling since October 2025 expansion announcement
- Nearly 50% of Claude usage in India focused on computer/mathematical tasks: application building, system modernization, production software

## Language & Technical Capabilities

- Launched company-wide initiative improving Claude performance in 10 major Indian languages: Hindi, Bengali, Marathi, Telugu, Tamil, Punjabi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Urdu
- Partnering with Karya and Collective Intelligence Project to build locally-relevant AI evaluations for agriculture and legal domains
- Created Model Context Protocol (MCP) as open-source standard, donated to Linux Foundation for AI system interoperability

## Enterprise Partnerships & Results

- Air India deploying Claude Code for faster, lower-cost custom software development across agentic AI operations
- CRED achieved 2x faster feature delivery and 10% better test coverage using Claude Code
- Cognizant rolling out Claude to 350,000 employees globally for legacy system modernization and software acceleration
- Razorpay integrating AI across risk systems, decision-making, and operations company-wide
- Emergent reached $25M ARR and 2M users in under 5 months building entirely with Claude

## Public Sector & Social Impact

- Pratham education nonprofit piloting Claude-powered Anytime Testing Machine with 1,500 students across 20 schools, expanding to 100 schools by 2026
- Educational tasks comprise 12% of Claude.ai usage in India
- Adalat AI launching national WhatsApp helpline using Claude for case updates, translation, and legal document processing in native languages
- EkStep Foundation partnership targeting population-scale AI impact in agriculture sector (16% of Indian economy, 50% of workforce)
- Indian Ministry of Statistics launched first official government MCP server for accessing national statistics

## Strategic Implications

- India's billion+ speakers across dozen+ languages represent massive untapped AI market opportunity
- Focus on locally-relevant applications (agriculture, legal, education) differentiates from generic AI deployments
- Open-source MCP adoption by government entities signals institutional AI integration pathway
- Enterprise customer base spans large corporations to startups, indicating broad market penetration potential
