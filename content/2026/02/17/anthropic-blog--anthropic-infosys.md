## Summary

## Product Announcements

- Partnership between Anthropic and Infosys to develop enterprise AI solutions for telecommunications, financial services, manufacturing, and software development
- Integration of Claude models and Claude Code with Infosys Topaz AI platform for regulated industries
- Launch of Claude Agent SDK for building persistent AI agents that handle multi-step tasks
- Introduction of Claude Cowork for automating enterprise operations tasks

## Technical Capabilities

- Agentic AI systems capable of independently processing claims, generating/testing code, and managing compliance reviews
- AI agents designed for long, complex tasks across regulated industries requiring precision and compliance
- Legacy system modernization tools combining Infosys Topaz and Claude for faster, cost-effective infrastructure updates
- Multi-cloud availability: Claude accessible on Amazon Bedrock, Google Cloud Vertex AI, and Microsoft Azure

## Strategic Implications

- India represents Anthropic's second-largest market with ~50% of usage focused on application development and production software
- Anthropic expanding India presence with Infosys as first major partner to tap into high-value developer market
- Focus on bridging gap between AI demos and production-ready solutions for regulated industries
- Targeting operationally complex sectors that demand domain expertise, governance, and transparency

## Actionable Insights

- Enterprises in regulated industries can leverage pre-built AI agents tailored to specific compliance and operational requirements
- Organizations with legacy systems should evaluate Claude-Infosys integration for modernization initiatives
- Software development teams can immediately access Claude Code to accelerate development cycles
- Companies should assess multi-cloud Claude deployment options based on existing infrastructure partnerships
