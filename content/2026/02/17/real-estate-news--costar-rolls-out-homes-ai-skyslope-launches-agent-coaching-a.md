## Summary

**CoStar Homes AI Launch**
- CoStar debuts conversational AI search tool on Homes.com using Microsoft Azure, allowing voice/text property searches in real time
- Tool integrates property database, Matterport 3D, school data, and market insights for comprehensive search experience
- Maintains "Your Listing, Your Lead" policy - all buyer inquiries still route directly to listing agents
- Plans to expand AI tool to other CoStar platforms beyond Homes.com

**SkySlope Agent Coaching App**
- Launches "Ayce" iOS app providing AI-powered goal-based coaching for real estate agents
- Converts annual income/production goals into personalized daily and weekly actionable tasks
- Includes real-time chat for prospecting advice, deal strategies, and market trend guidance
- Offers customizable coaching tone preferences and execution scripts/templates

**GPAR Safety Tool Implementation**
- Greater Philadelphia Association of Realtors introduces Tether RE safety monitoring platform
- Features 24/7 live monitoring, SOS alerts, proximity timers, and impact/struggle detection
- Includes productivity tools: turn-by-turn navigation and mileage/expense tracking
- Rolling out next month as member benefit

**OneKey MLS-Rayse Partnership**
- Rayse partners with OneKey MLS to serve 43,000+ New York metro area agents
- Provides agent transparency platform and educational tools to articulate professional value
- Focuses on helping agents communicate impact and set clear client expectations
- Scheduled deployment for early 2026

**Strategic Implications**
- AI integration becoming standard across multiple real estate technology platforms
- Industry prioritizing agent safety and professional development tools
- Technology companies emphasizing agent value preservation while enhancing consumer experience
- Regional MLS organizations actively seeking differentiated member benefits through strategic partnerships
