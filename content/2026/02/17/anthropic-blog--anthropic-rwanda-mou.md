## Summary

- **First government MOU in Africa**: Anthropic signs three-year Memorandum of Understanding with Rwanda Government, marking first formalized multi-sector partnership on the continent

- **Health sector expansion**: Claude AI will support Ministry of Health's cervical cancer elimination plan and efforts to reduce malaria and maternal mortality rates

- **Public sector AI integration**: Government developer teams receive Claude and Claude Code access, plus hands-on training, capacity building, and API credits for broader AI adoption

- **Education partnership formalization**: Codifies fall 2025 agreement including 2,000 Claude Pro licenses for Rwandan educators and AI literacy training for public servants

- **Regional AI learning companion**: Claude-powered educational tool deployed across eight African countries beyond Rwanda

- **Beneficial Deployments team strategy**: Anthropic's dedicated team designs programs specifically matched to Rwanda's national needs and priorities

- **Capacity building focus**: Investment in training, technical support, and skills development to enable independent AI use by teachers, healthcare workers, and government employees

- **Strategic market positioning**: Partnership demonstrates Anthropic's commitment to public sector deployments and expansion into emerging markets with government backing

- **Responsible deployment model**: Emphasizes local autonomy, capacity building, and responsible introduction of AI technologies in critical sectors

- **Cross-sector approach**: Multi-domain strategy spanning health, education, and governance creates potential template for future government partnerships
