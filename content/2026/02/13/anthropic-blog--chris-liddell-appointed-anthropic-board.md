## Summary

- Chris Liddell appointed to Anthropic's Board of Directors with 30+ years senior leadership experience at Microsoft (CFO), General Motors (CFO), International Paper (CFO), and White House Deputy Chief of Staff

- Board composition now includes Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, Yasmin Razavi, Jay Kreps, Reed Hastings, and Liddell

- Liddell brings government technology experience as former Director of American Technology Council focused on modernizing government technology

- Current directorships include Commonwealth Fusion Systems and Council on Foreign Relations, indicating continued involvement in emerging technologies and policy

- Appointment signals Anthropic's focus on governance and regulatory compliance as AI impact scales

- Strategic emphasis on intersection of technology, public service, and governance during high-stakes periods

- Anthropic operates as Public Benefit Corporation with board elected by stockholders and Long-Term Benefit Trust

- Company recently raised $30 billion Series G at $380 billion valuation with $14 billion run-rate revenue growing 10x annually

- Expansion activities include new Bengaluru office, India partnerships, and CodePath collaboration for US collegiate computer science programs

- Positioning as "market leader in enterprise AI and coding" with focus on responsible AI development
