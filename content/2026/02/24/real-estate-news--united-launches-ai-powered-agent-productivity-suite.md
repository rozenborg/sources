## Summary

- United Real Estate launches BullseyeAI, a free AI productivity suite for affiliates combining large language models, conversational AI, and automated agents in one dashboard

- BullseyeAI automates time-consuming tasks via voice/text commands: contact management, follow-up drafting, lead assignment, saved searches, and email campaigns

- RealReports becomes official tax records provider for Imagine MLS's 4,000+ subscribers in Kentucky, deploying Taxshot and Prospector platforms

- Taxshot consolidates nationwide public records and tax data into standardized system, eliminating platform switching for ownership verification and property analysis

- Zillow partners with Google NotebookLM to provide homebuying guidance through dedicated AI research notebook with expert-backed responses and citations

- ROAM MLS (Louisiana) selects Flexmls platform to serve 13,000 subscribers statewide, consolidating previously fragmented systems across four Realtor associations

- Flexmls automates listing input by populating trusted property and media sources, reducing data entry time while maintaining accuracy

- HomeAgeUpdateGuide.com launches as free educational resource providing standardized reference for home component life expectancy and replacement timing

- New site addresses gap in automated valuation models (AVMs) regarding home update data, targeting lenders, appraisers, and real estate professionals

- AI integration across real estate platforms accelerating, with focus on workflow automation, data consolidation, and reducing manual tasks

- MLS consolidation trend continues as regional organizations seek unified technology platforms to serve larger subscriber bases

- Industry moving toward embedding AI guidance directly into existing consumer research tools rather than creating standalone applications
