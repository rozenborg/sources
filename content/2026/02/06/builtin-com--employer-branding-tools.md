## Summary

## Key Product Announcements

- Built In launched AI-powered Employer Brand Reputation (EBR) Score platform - first solution to measure how AI tools like ChatGPT represent companies to job seekers
- EBR Score analyzes hundreds of prompts across 7 core areas: Career Growth, Work-Life Balance, Compensation, Leadership, Stability, Culture, and Workplace Perception
- Built In platform combines reputation scoring, AI-optimized content creation, and hiring tools in single solution
- Platform provides actionable recommendations including content correction, strength amplification, and full-service consulting

## Technical Capabilities

- Built In uses hundreds of AI prompts to simulate actual candidate questions and generate comprehensive reputation reports
- LinkedIn offers analytics dashboards for engagement metrics and audience segmentation
- Symphony Talent's SmashFlyX integrates recruitment marketing with candidate relationship management and multi-channel analytics
- Phenom creates personalized career experiences connecting employer content, job discovery, and candidate communication
- Hibob centralizes people analytics dashboards to track engagement and retention metrics

## Strategic Market Context

- U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics reports elevated quit rates in knowledge and tech sectors
- Job seekers increasingly use AI tools (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity) to guide career decisions
- Digital transparency has raised candidate expectations - they consult anonymous reviews, social media, and third-party rankings
- Employer branding now critical component of long-term hiring success, not peripheral initiative

## Platform Ecosystem Analysis

- **Review Management**: Glassdoor, Kununu, Comparably, Fairygodboss enable reputation monitoring and review response
- **Early Career Focus**: Handshake, Yello target student and recent graduate segments through university partnerships
- **Content-Driven**: The Muse uses multimedia storytelling with employee interviews and workplace tours
- **Employee Advocacy**: PageUp's PathMotion enables direct candidate-to-employee Q&A interactions
- **Global Intelligence**: Universum provides benchmarking data across 70+ markets for localization strategies

## Actionable Insights for Companies

- Audit current AI representation using tools like Built In's EBR Score before competitors gain advantage
- Implement employee advocacy programs through platforms like PageUp PathMotion to provide authentic candidate interactions
- Establish early career talent pipelines via Handshake and Yello before candidates enter competitive job market
- Use Universum's global data to develop localized employer value propositions for international expansion
- Leverage multimedia storytelling through The Muse to differentiate from standard job postings and company descriptions
