MoxiWorks launches an enhanced RISE platform that uses AI to automatically identify the five most important contacts agents should prioritize each day, moving beyond basic reactive tools to drive complete business workflows. The update includes integrated marketing tools and mobile alerts designed to help real estate professionals maintain consistent client follow-up and convert leads more effectively.

---

- **Proactive lead prioritization** The new "Top 5 Contacts" feature uses AI to analyze engagement, activity and relationship signals to surface the most promising prospects daily, complete with relevant client information like property preferences

- **Mobile workflow integration** RISE connects to a companion app that sends priority alerts to agents on-the-go, ensuring critical follow-up tasks aren't missed while away from the desk

- **Marketing automation bundle** Two-way Canva integration pulls listing data directly into branded content creation, while the proprietary Promote tool automatically converts listings into targeted social media advertisements

- **Continuous enhancement cycle** MoxiWorks plans monthly feature rollouts throughout 2026, signaling sustained investment in platform development rather than a one-time update

- **Workflow completion focus** The platform emphasizes moving agents from planning to execution faster, addressing the common industry challenge of inconsistent client follow-up that leads to lost opportunities

- **Strategic repositioning** CEO Eric Elfman frames this as proof of internal transformation and "self-disruption," suggesting MoxiWorks is pivoting from traditional PropTech tools toward comprehensive business automation

- **Competitive differentiation** By emphasizing "beyond reactive AI," MoxiWorks positions itself against competitors offering basic chatbots or simple automation, targeting brokerages seeking integrated workflow solutions
