PwC is deploying Claude across its global workforce of hundreds of thousands to transform enterprise operations for clients, with production deployments already cutting delivery times by up to 70% in critical sectors like insurance underwriting and cybersecurity. This represents Anthropic's deepest partnership commitment, targeting the estimated $2 trillion drag from pre-AI enterprise systems through AI-native rebuilds of core business functions.

---

- **Global workforce deployment** PwC rolling out Claude Code and Cowork starting in US then expanding globally, with plans to train and certify 30,000 professionals through a joint Center of Excellence

- **Production results proven** Live deployments show insurance underwriting compressed from 10 weeks to 10 days, cybersecurity incident response from hours to minutes, and overall delivery improvements up to 70%

- **Three strategic focus areas** Agentic technology builds shipping production software in weeks instead of quarters, AI-native deal execution for private equity, and complete reinvention of enterprise functions like finance and HR

- **New business unit launch** PwC creating standalone "Office of the CFO" practice built entirely on Claude, starting with regulated industries where accuracy and auditability are critical

- **High-stakes sector penetration** Active in healthcare (167,000-person Advocate Health deployment), financial services, life sciences meeting FDA/EMA standards, and cybersecurity where accuracy is non-negotiable

- **Scale advantage demonstrated** COBOL mainframe modernization handling codebase 4x larger than scoped while staying on time and under budget, plus HR transformation delivering working prototype in one week

- **Customer Zero validation** Both PwC and Anthropic used Claude internally before client deployment, with PwC using it for journal entries and annual planning optimization while helping scale Anthropic's own CFO operations
