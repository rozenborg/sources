Anthropic has secured access to SpaceX's entire Colossus 1 data center providing over 220,000 NVIDIA GPUs within a month, part of massive compute expansion that enables doubled usage limits for Claude Code and significantly higher API rate limits. The company is positioning for orbital AI compute development while building international capacity in democratic countries to serve regulated enterprise customers.

---

- **Immediate capacity boost** SpaceX partnership delivers 300+ megawatts of compute power (220,000+ NVIDIA GPUs) within 30 days, directly improving Claude Pro and Max subscriber experience

- **Usage limit increases** Claude Code rate limits doubled across Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise plans, with peak hours restrictions removed for Pro and Max accounts, plus substantial API rate limit increases for Claude Opus models

- **Orbital computing ambitions** Partnership includes expressed interest in developing multi-gigawatt orbital AI compute capacity with SpaceX, signaling potential space-based AI infrastructure development

- **Massive compute pipeline** Total announced capacity exceeds 15 gigawatts across partnerships with Amazon (5GW), Google/Broadcom (5GW), Microsoft/NVIDIA ($30B Azure), and Fluidstack ($50B investment)

- **International expansion strategy** Targeting democratic countries with strong legal frameworks for regulated enterprise customers in financial services, healthcare, and government requiring data residency compliance

- **Community investment commitment** Pledging to cover consumer electricity price increases from US data centers and exploring similar commitments internationally while investing in local communities

- **Multi-platform hardware strategy** Operating across AWS Trainium, Google TPUs, and NVIDIA GPUs to diversify compute dependencies and optimize performance across different workloads
