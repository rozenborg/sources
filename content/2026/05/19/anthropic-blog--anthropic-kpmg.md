KPMG has formed a global strategic alliance with Anthropic to deploy Claude AI across its entire 276,000-person workforce and core client platforms, marking one of the largest enterprise AI implementations to date. The partnership extends beyond internal use to joint product development for private equity clients and integration into KPMG's primary Digital Gateway platform where tax and legal work is performed.

---

- **Massive enterprise deployment** KPMG will provide Claude access to all 276,000+ employees globally, building on two years of US adoption and representing unprecedented scale for professional services AI integration

- **Core platform integration** Claude Cowork and Managed Agents are embedded directly into Digital Gateway (built on Microsoft Azure), KPMG's primary platform where client work, proprietary tools, and data converge

- **Dramatic efficiency gains** Building AI agents for tasks like tax regulation compliance dropped from weeks of multi-tool workflows to minutes within the integrated platform, fundamentally changing work processes

- **Private equity focus** Anthropic designated KPMG as preferred partner for PE firms, with new offerings like KPMG Blaze using Claude Code to modernize portfolio company IT systems and accelerate AI-enabled product development

- **Cybersecurity applications** The alliance will use Claude to identify and remediate vulnerabilities in critical systems, guided by KPMG's Trusted AI framework for high-stakes security work

- **Joint research insights** Collaboration with UT Austin's McCombs School reveals that AI value depends heavily on human judgment, workflow design, and decision-making rather than just technology adoption

- **Revenue expansion opportunity** The partnership positions both companies to capture growing demand for AI transformation in audit, tax, legal, and advisory services across KPMG's 138-country footprint
