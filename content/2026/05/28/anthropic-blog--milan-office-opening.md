Anthropic opens its sixth European office in Milan to support Italian enterprise adoption and contribute to broader societal conversations about AI development, building on existing partnerships with major Italian companies like Generali, Enel, and Pirelli that have already demonstrated significant productivity gains from Claude deployment.

---

- **Enterprise traction** Italian companies across finance (Generali, Unipol), pharma (Angelini, Bracco), energy (Enel), and automotive (Pirelli) are already using Claude, indicating strong market penetration before formal office opening

- **Proven productivity gains** Satispay compressed an 18-month roadmap into seven months and updated core payment systems 10x faster; JAKALA freed up 70% of senior team time across 3,000 seats

- **Developer adoption momentum** Bending Spoons reports majority of code changes now co-authored with Claude Code, suggesting deep technical integration in Italian tech companies

- **Cultural positioning strategy** Anthropic leveraged Pope Leo XIV's AI encyclical presentation to position itself in Italy's broader societal AI discourse, with co-founder Chris Olah speaking on AI ethics

- **Creative industry expansion** Partnership with Alcova Milano during Design Week targets Italy's design sector, extending beyond traditional enterprise use cases into creative workflows

- **Regional leadership structure** Thomas Remy leads Southern Europe operations, indicating broader Mediterranean expansion strategy beyond just Italy

- **Safety-first messaging** Emphasis on "safe AI transition" and multi-stakeholder dialogue positions Anthropic as responsible AI leader in regulatory-conscious European market
