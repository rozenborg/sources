Anthropic has secured a massive $65 billion Series H funding round at a $965 billion valuation, positioning the AI company as one of the world's most valuable private firms while reporting $47 billion in run-rate revenue just months after their previous raise. The funding will fuel aggressive compute expansion through new partnerships with Amazon, Google, and SpaceX totaling 10+ gigawatts of capacity, as Claude becomes the first frontier AI model available across all three major cloud platforms.

---

- **Valuation surge** $965B post-money valuation represents exponential growth since Series G in February, reflecting rapid enterprise adoption and revenue scaling
- **Revenue momentum** $47B annual run-rate revenue demonstrates strong commercial traction across global enterprise customers deploying Claude in core operations
- **Compute expansion** Secured 10+ gigawatts of new capacity through Amazon (5GW), Google/Broadcom TPUs (5GW), and SpaceX Colossus GPU access to meet surging demand
- **Strategic hardware partnerships** Micron, Samsung, and SK Hynix investments provide critical supply chain security for memory, storage, and logic chips amid AI chip shortages
- **Multi-cloud dominance** Claude now available on AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure, giving Anthropic unprecedented distribution reach across enterprise customers
- **Product development focus** Funding will advance safety research, interpretability capabilities, and expand Claude Code and Cowork tools for professional workflows
- **Blue-chip investor backing** Round led by tier-1 VCs and includes $15B from hyperscalers, signaling institutional confidence in Anthropic's competitive position against OpenAI
- **European expansion** New Milan office reflects growing international enterprise demand and regulatory compliance needs in key markets
