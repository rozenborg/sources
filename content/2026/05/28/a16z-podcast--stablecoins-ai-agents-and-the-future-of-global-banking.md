Jeeves is building a stablecoin-powered financial operating system across 25 Latin American countries, leveraging AI to automate underwriting and compliance while processing billions in payment volume. The company sees stablecoins as the infrastructure layer that will enable true global banking by solving currency volatility and cross-border friction that traditional banking cannot address at scale.

---

- **Stablecoin adoption driver** Latin America's currency volatility and inflation make USD stablecoins attractive for businesses seeking stability, with regulatory frameworks becoming more accommodating across the region

- **AI-powered scaling** Jeeves uses AI agents to automate underwriting decisions, customer support, transaction reconciliation, and KYB (Know Your Business) processes, enabling rapid expansion without proportional headcount growth

- **Multi-country complexity** Operating across 25 countries requires navigating different regulatory frameworks, payment rails, and compliance requirements, making localized infrastructure critical for enterprise customers

- **Global banking vision** Stablecoins represent the foundational layer for a truly global financial system that can bypass traditional correspondent banking limitations and currency conversion costs

- **Enterprise focus** Jeeves targets businesses needing cross-border payments and multi-currency operations, positioning itself as financial infrastructure rather than a consumer fintech

- **Regulatory navigation** Success requires building relationships with local regulators and adapting to evolving stablecoin regulations while maintaining compliance across jurisdictions

- **Payment volume scale** The company processes billions in payment volume, indicating significant enterprise adoption of stablecoin-based financial services in emerging markets
