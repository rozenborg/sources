Anthropic is establishing a Seoul office under new Korea Representative Director KiYoung Choi to capitalize on exceptionally high Claude adoption rates, with Koreans using the AI assistant at 3.5 times the expected rate and major enterprises like SK Telecom already deploying custom Claude solutions.

---

- **Market penetration** Korea shows outsized Claude.ai usage at 3.5x population-expected rates, concentrated in technical and creative applications, signaling strong product-market fit
- **Leadership hire** KiYoung Choi brings 30+ years of enterprise technology experience from Snowflake, Google Cloud, Adobe, and Microsoft across Korea and APAC markets
- **Enterprise adoption** SK Telecom deployed Claude for custom AI customer service while Law&Company uses it for legal research and document preparation, demonstrating enterprise-grade use cases
- **Strategic positioning** Anthropic targets Korea's "sophisticated AI market" known for hardware innovation and responsible AI deployment practices that align with company values
- **Go-to-market focus** Seoul office will pursue enterprise partnerships, government/research engagement, and developer community support rather than just consumer adoption
- **Expansion signal** Senior Anthropic leadership visiting Seoul for official opening indicates significant strategic investment in Asian markets beyond initial presence
