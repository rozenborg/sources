Anthropic is launching ten pre-built AI agent templates that automate core financial workflows like building pitchbooks, KYC screening, and month-end closing, while expanding Claude's integration across Microsoft Office applications with persistent context. The company is positioning itself as the comprehensive AI infrastructure for financial services through new data partnerships and managed agent capabilities that can run autonomously or alongside human analysts.

---

- **Ready-to-deploy templates** cover both front office (pitch building, earnings review, market research) and back office (GL reconciliation, statement auditing, month-end closing) with reference architectures including skills, connectors, and subagents
- **Microsoft 365 integration** enables Claude to work natively in Excel, PowerPoint, Word, and Outlook with context that carries automatically between applications, eliminating need to re-explain work across platforms
- **Dual deployment options** allow templates to run as desktop plugins alongside analysts or as autonomous managed agents on Claude Platform for book-wide operations and scheduled workflows
- **Expanded data ecosystem** adds eight new connectors including Dun & Bradstreet, FactSet, S&P Capital IQ, and others, plus Moody's MCP app providing interactive UI for 600+ million company profiles
- **Enterprise adoption momentum** shows 100% deployment at Walleye Capital's 400-person hedge fund and implementations across major institutions like Carlyle, FIS, and unnamed banks/asset managers
- **Technical advancement** powered by Claude Opus 4.7 achieving 64.37% on Vals AI's Finance Agent benchmark, positioning as state-of-the-art for financial tasks
- **Compliance-ready architecture** includes audit logs, per-tool permissions, credential vaults, and human-in-the-loop approval workflows required for regulated financial environments
