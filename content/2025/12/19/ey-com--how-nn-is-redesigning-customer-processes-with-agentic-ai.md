## Summary

- **Product Announcements:**
  - Developing AI-agent powered mortgage application processing available 24/7 (targeting off-hours customer needs)
  - Implementing AI-agents for insurance claims processing and customer inquiries
  - Building toward "two-minute company" model - complete customer transactions in minutes

- **Technical Capabilities:**
  - Deploying AI-agents selectively for complex processes requiring intelligence (avoiding simple decision trees)
  - Leveraging Large Language Models for customer-facing applications
  - Redesigning both customer service and backend processes using agentic AI

- **Strategic Implications:**
  - AI adoption is mandatory, not optional, to meet rising customer expectations set by consumer LLM experiences
  - Technology provides first-mover advantage only - not sustainable competitive differentiation
  - Success depends on human-AI interaction quality, not just technology deployment

- **Actionable Insights:**
  - Implement organization-wide AI literacy training (NN training 16,000 employees)
  - Focus AI deployment on complex processes where intelligence adds value
  - Accelerate lifelong learning programs to support AI transformation
  - Prioritize speed-to-market for first-mover advantages in financial services
  - Design customer experiences around 24/7 availability and rapid transaction completion
